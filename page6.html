Tell me About Your  Favorite Application




Hello there everyone, im here to present you my favorite application which is grab.

Can you imagine yourself stuck in a place where you can’t see any vehicles or mode of transportation, well with this application you can now book a ride with the type of car you want easier, safer and convenient. Grab is a Singapore-based technology that offers transport services, food delivery and payment solutions. This application is not just about transportation and all. It also comes with a food deliver that covers all the restaurants here in the Philippines. If you feel lazy enough or having some hard time to visit a mall, you can book grabfood, then you will be able to pick the food that you want from any restaurants or fast food chain in our country. Also, this application has its payment methods and now you can go and be cashless with its own grabpay.

You can also use your other cards as a payment for your ride. See its easy and convenient.  
I discovered this application with some of my friends back in high school, then I started to download this app, and use it as my mode of transportation when I really can’t find any transportation in the place that I'm in.
