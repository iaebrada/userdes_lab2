Reflection #1
1st Week of Userdes


In this week of our USERDS Class, we had an introduction with our professor, and we tackled about the design process. In line with this, we also had our first activity that is called “Tell me about yourself” in this activity, our professor had the chance to know us deeper about our background, likes, hobbies and what are our expectations on this subject.

To tell you about the design process, I will tell you the operation involved in this process. First and for most, you can’t use this process with out the problem, so you need to have a problem. Next will be the collection of information or data for you to have a background on your problem. Then, here comes the brainstorming and analyzation of the problem, on this process the person itself is thinking of what he/she would do to his/her problem or what are the possible ways to solve this one. Furthermore, the next step would be the solutions or development of a model. In this step, he/she would create a possible solution for the problem. Moreover, the second to the last step would be the presentation of your ideas to others, so that you can hear from others on what are their ideas or knowledge about the problem. Lastly, the improvement of your design. In the last step of the process, the improvement of the design is where the person is developing his/her design to enhance the work.
